Know some of our feature !!!

Mute/Unmute

If you want to mute yourself, tap or click on the Audio On/Off icon in the bottom part of the screen.By that way, you are still audible to the caller but they are not audible to you. Similarly, if you want to speak and make yourself audible to other person in a call, then you can click the same button to unmute yourself.

Video off/On

If you want to make sure that your camera isn’t recording, tap or click the video On/Off icon in the bottom part of the screen.That button makes sure that your camera is off.Similarly if you want to make sure that your camera is recording you then you can click the same button to turn on your camera.

Share Screen

If you want to share your screen tap or click the Share screen icon in the bottom part of the screen.Screen sharing involves sharing access to a given computer screen.Through screen sharing you can quickly share your presentations, holiday photos or anything on your screen during a call.
